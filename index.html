Sources & Methodology

AI Investment based on reported capex from Alphabet, Microsoft, Amazon, and Meta earnings disclosures (Big 4). Big 4 represent ~80–85% of tracked AI infrastructure spend; totals scaled ×1.15 to approximate full market. Historical context: Stanford HAI AI Index 2025 and Goldman Sachs AI build-out model. 2026 Big 4 guidance ~$725B; 2027+ from analyst consensus (BofA, Evercore); 2031 plateau from Goldman baseline ~$1.6T.

Category split (approximate industry analysis): Hardware/GPUs/servers 45%, Data centre construction/power/cooling 40%, Software & other 15%. Depreciation straight-line: Software 3yr, Hardware 4yr, Data Centres 30yr. Net Book Value = cumulative undepreciated asset stock across all cohorts.

Global GFCF (Gross Fixed Capital Formation): World Bank / IMF data, ~24% of world GDP (~$105T in 2024 → GFCF ~$26T). Projected at ~5% nominal growth. Global produced capital stock ~$520T (McKinsey MGI 2024).

Population & AI users: World population from UN medium-variant projection. AI user base extrapolated from smartphone penetration (6.8B in 2024), converging toward saturation ~2033. 2024 active AI users baseline ~1.5B.
